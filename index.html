About Us

Anna Dudarewicz

Anna has been working in the industry for 24 years. She trained in Europe, specifically, Poland and Germany. She founded three successful salons since coming to Canada.

Anna has trained with some of the industries finest. She continues her education constantly, making sure that she stays current with techniques and trends.

Alexa Dudarewicz

Alexa is a graduate from the Fiorio Beauty Academy. She trained among major names in the industry. Since graduating, Alexa has attended several courses including, but not limited to, advanced cutting, makeup, and barbering.

Alexa is a colour correction specialist, and strives to help you achieve your colour goals. She continues her education, staying current with products and trends to better address your needs.
